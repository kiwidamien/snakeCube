Snake on a planar cube!

Below is the classic game "Snake" on a flattened cube. Cells of the same color are on the same cube face.

Instructions

  • Press 'F' to move the snake in its current direction
  • Press 'L' or 'R' to rotate the snake's head left or right
  • Click on any empty square (_) to toggle food (o)
  • If the snake dies, refresh the browser
